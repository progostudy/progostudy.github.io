No, I don't expect full consensus between or among people for every topic because of these following reasons.

Full consensus is barely achievable even when it is being tried in a certain society which is homogenous, let alone among people from different eras or culture. This is because people hold different values and beliefs that can be valid in itself. in addition, different eras call for different values as well.

What was right back in 1500s might be perceived as wrong or weird in 2000s, and this is a fact. So, considering that history is a never-rending dialogue of events between the past and the present, it would be relatively okay even if no full consensus were never reached among people. At the very least, we can compare how they lived in a certain era without agreeing or bringing their values back to the present times, and this in itself is what makes civilization beautiful.
