28. how does the word amen express to jesus in assay form​


Amen is generally used after a prayer, belief, or other formal expression. It shall be spoken in order to express solemn ratification or agreement. It means "that's it" or "that's it." Amen is derived from the Hebrew āmēn, which means "certainty," "fact," and "truly." Amen is contained in both the Old and New Testaments.

The Christian. The use of "amen" has traditionally been accepted in Christian worship as a closing word for prayers and hymns and an expression of deep agreement. Jesus also used amen to stress his own words.
