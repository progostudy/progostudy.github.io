26. 4. Lloyd takes a 25-minute nap. He wakes up at 3:40 p.m. What time does Lloyd take his nap? 25 min 3:40 p.m.


To find out what time Lloyd takes his nap, you can subtract the duration of his nap from the time he wakes up.

25 minutes is the same as 25 minutes/60 minutes = 0.416666 hours.

So if Lloyd wakes up at 3:40 p.m., we can subtract 0.416666 hours from 3:40 p.m. which gives 3:40 p.m. - 0.416666 hours = 3:25 p.m.

So Lloyd takes his nap at 3:25 p.m.
