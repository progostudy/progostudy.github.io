5. 3 days ago I just turned 18, next year I will turn 20, when is my birthday?


Answer:

If three (3) days ago you just turned 18, and next year you will turn 20. Your birthday is January 1.

Explanation:

If three (3) days ago you just turned 18, and next year you will turn 20. Your birthday is January 1.

This is a word problem. The age of the user three (3) days ago is 18. Having said that, the user's present age is 19 years old. And then next year, the user's age is 20.

If you analyze clearly, the user's birthday is near the end of every year and if we assume January 1, the age of the user is 18 years old three (3) days ago (and three days ago is December 29).

Now having said that, the user's age is 18 years old on December 29 and three days later( which is January 1) the user will turn 19 years old. And again next year, January 1 (which is again the user's birthday) he/she will turn to 20 years old.  Remember that we always celebrate our birthdays every year.

20. 3 days ago I just turned 18, next year I will turn 20, when is my birthday?​


Answer:

Explanation:

Word Problem

In order to know what date the writer's birthday is, let's first list down the given facts of the word problem.

Given: 3 days ago, writer's age is 18 years oldThus, Present age of the writer is 19 years old. Next year, the writer's age is 20 years old.

Required:

Birth date

Solution:

Logically speaking, the writer's birthdate is someday near the end of the year, possibly  Jan 1.

Let's look into the possibility,

Assuming the writer's birthday is January 1, the writer's age is 18 years old  3 days ago.

The date 3 days before January 1 is, December 29.

Thus, on December 29, the writer's age is 18 years old.

3 days later, January 1 which the first day of a new year, the writer turned 19 years old.

Next year, January 1 the writer will turn 20 years old.  

Let's check if all the given facts are satisfied.

3 days ago, writer's age is 18 years old: December 29 Present age of the writer is 19 years old. : January 1 current yearNext year, the writer's age is 20 years old.: January 1 next year

Since All conditions are satisfied,

it can therefore be concluded that the writer's birthday is January 1.
