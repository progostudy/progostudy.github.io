Spirituality can provide comfort, guidance, and purpose in life.Don't miss anything - This refers to living life to the fullest and making the most of every opportunity. It means pursuing one's passions, taking risks, and stepping out of one's comfort zone.Inspire your family and the inner circle - This means being a positive influence on those closest to you. It includes setting a good example, being supportive, and encouraging others to pursue their own dreams and goals.

In summary, a good life is one that involves being productive, cultivating positive relationships, preserving cultural identity, nurturing spirituality, seizing opportunities, and inspiring others.

Answer:

D. Inertia

Explanation:

This is an example of centripetal (circular) motion. The water stays in the bucket because of inertia. The water wants to fly off from the circle, but the bucket gets in the way and keeps it in place. This is the same effect you feel when you go around a tight corner in the car and get squished against the door.
