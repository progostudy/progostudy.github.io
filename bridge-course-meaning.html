22. What does "bridge" mean in sports guide? heres the homework we have I just wanna know the meaning: "Research the meaning of "bridge" in the sports guide. Write on half of the paper"


Answer:

In sports, "bridge" usually refers to a technique used in billiards or pool, where the player uses their bridge hand (the hand used to steady the cue) to create a stable platform for the cue stick to slide smoothly through. The bridge hand is placed on the table, and the player uses their fingers to form a V-shape or an open bridge, depending on the shot they want to take. The cue stick is then rested on the bridge, allowing the player to accurately strike the cue ball. The bridge is an important part of the game, as it helps players maintain their balance and control over the cue stick, which is essential for making accurate shots.
