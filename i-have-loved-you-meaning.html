Answer:

1) The saying "It is better to give than to receive" implies that it is more fulfilling to be the giver rather than the receiver in any kind of transaction. However, this can be seen as contradictory as sometimes receiving can bring joy and happiness to people's lives.

2) The quote by Mother Teresa highlights the paradoxical nature of love, where the more love you give, the more love you receive. It suggests that love has the power to overcome any hurt or pain, and that by loving selflessly, you can find more love and happiness.

This can be seen as contradictory as it implies that in order for something to live, it must first die.
