16. bakit mahalagang mapangalagaan at maprotektahan ang biodiversity sa asia?


Biodiversity is described as the variety of life in a specific area. It is important to maintain a high level of index of diversity in places especially in Asia because the biodiversity of organisms helps in maintaining a balance ecosystem. It is also important because it helps in withstanding environmental changes that spontaneously occur just like in the Asian countries. Asia is prone to typhoons and other calamities so in order for ecosystems to survive, a high level of biodiversity must be maintained to be able to cope up to the changes in the environment.
