16. history, description,usage of balanghayhistory, description,usage of vinta​


Balangay, also spelled barangay, is a type of lashed-lug boat built by joining planks edge-to-edge using pins, dowels, and fiber lashings. They are found throughout the Philippines and were used largely as trading ships up until the colonial era.

Vinta are characterized by their colorful rectangular lug sails and bifurcated prows and sterns, which resemble the gaping mouth of a crocodile. Vinta are used as fishing vessels, cargo ships, and houseboats. Smaller undecorated versions of the vinta used for fishing are known as tondaan.

22. usage of motherboard​


Answer:

The motherboard acts as a single platform for connecting all of the components of a computer. It directly or via cables connects the CPU, memory, hard drives, optical drives, video card, sound card, and other ports and expansion cards. It is often referred to as a computer's "backbone."
