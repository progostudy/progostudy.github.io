Answer:

Question:

The barangay street cleaner is assigned to clean 3/4 of the whole Laurel St. if he cleaned 2/3 of it on that day, how much was left to clean on the next day?

Answer:

What is asked in the problem?

How much was left to clean on the next day?

What is given facts?

¾ clean of the whole Laurel St.

⅔ cleaned of it on that day

What operation to be used in the problem?

Subraction

What is the number sentence?

¾ - ⅔ = n

What is the Solution?

[tex] \frac{3}{4} \times \frac{3}{3} = \frac{9}{12} \\ \frac{2}{3} \times \frac{4}{4} = \frac{8}{12} \\ \frac{9}{12} - \frac{8}{12} = \frac{1}{12} [/tex]

What is the final answer?

1/12 was left to clean on the next day.
