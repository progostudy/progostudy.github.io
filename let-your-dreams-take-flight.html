Answer:

Your friends might be taking into account the time difference between Manila and your destination. If your destination is located in a country with a time zone that is two hours behind the Philippines, then even though the actual travel time is three hours, you will arrive at your destination five hours after your departure time from Manila. This is because the clock in your destination will show a time that is two hours earlier than the time in Manila, despite the fact that only three hours have passed since you left Manila. Therefore, your friends might have factored in the time difference when they estimated that the flight will take five hours.

ROLES/DUTIESWhat is the main role of a flight attendant?

Flight Attendants are generally responsible for ensuring passengers' safety and comfort at all times. Their duties include ensuring that the emergency equipment is working, that the cabin is clean, and that there is an adequate supply of food and beverages.

Flight Attendant job description: Job duties and responsibilitiesdescription should contain the following duties and responsibilities:

Always conduct safety check before flight.

Greet and communicate with customers.

Help passengers find their seats.

Prepare and serve drinks and food to passengers.

Present emergency equipment and give instructions to passengers on how to use this equipment

Monitor, manage and secure the cabin.

Adhere to all aviation rules and regulations.

Assist passengers and cabin crew during emergency situations.

Provide help to passengers with special needs (children, disabled persons, elders etc)

Prepare and submit reports on flight incidents.

30. Specific Measurable Achievable- Relevant- . Time-bounded example for Dream to be a flight attendance ​


Answer:

Here's an example of a SMART goal for someone who dreams of becoming a flight attendant:

Specific: I want to become a flight attendant for a major airline company.

Measurable: I will submit my job application to at least 5 major airline companies within the next 3 months.

Achievable: I have completed the required education and training to become a flight attendant, and I have gained relevant work experience in customer service, which is a valuable skill for this job.

Relevant: Becoming a flight attendant is aligned with my personal interests, strengths, and career goals.

Time-bound: I will secure a job offer as a flight attendant within the next 6 months.

By setting a SMART goal like this, the individual has a clear and specific target to work towards. They have identified the steps they need to take (submitting job applications) and a timeframe for achieving their goal. Additionally, they have ensured that their goal is achievable and relevant to their interests and skills. This goal can be measured by the number of job applications submitted and the time it takes to secure a job offer.
