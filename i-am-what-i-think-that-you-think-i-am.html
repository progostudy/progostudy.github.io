10. i am thinking of a number. if you add 47 to it and subtract 19 from the sum,the result is 85. what number am i thinking of?


The answer is 57.

19+85 = 104
104 - 47 = 57

Checking of answer:
1. If you add 47 to it:
47+57 = 104

2. Subtract 19 from the sum:
104-19 = 85

Let the number be x:

(x + 47) - 19 = 85

x = 85 - 47 + 19

x = (85 + 19) - 47

x = 104 - 47

x = 57

ANSWER: The number is 57.
