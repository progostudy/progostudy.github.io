Participants

In every meeting or discussion, other than the speaker and the organizers the participants also have responsibilities while listening. They need to participate and be cooperative to the speakers as it will benefit with the flow of the discussion and is easier to come up with new ideas by not just being physically present but also mentally.

The answers from the selection are:

Listening to the people who are speaking and taking notes if ever needed Responding to the questions asked by the speaker or the leader Speaking and sharing personal opinions and thoughts about the certain topic being discussed

Other than the answers from the selection the participants should also:

Be cooperative if there is a small activity or energizer for the sake of keeping the interest levels and mental presence of the participants If possible be skeptic or contemplate on the facts to correct it if wrong and entertain or accept it if right

The participants are the main people that a discussion is focusing on, since they are to give enlightening ideas or information to them or by sharing previous outcomes of other things to give rise in an open discussion where the audience is free to give their constructive criticism or to give their own view of things.

26. does sport help us to have a healthy mental state of mind how​


Answer:

Yes It help us to have a mental state of mind.

Explanation:

Sports helps you manage stress. Exercise causes your body to release endorphins, the chemicals in your brain that relieve pain and stress. It also reduces the levels of stress hormones, cortisol and adrenaline. Studies have shown that 20 to 30 minutes of exercise each day can make people feel calmer.

HOW do sports affect mental health?
Sports are associated with lower rates of stress, anxiety, depression, and suicidal behavior. Participation in team sports reduces the risk of teen substance abuse and other reckless behaviors. Team sports enhance resilience, empathy, confidence and empowerment.
