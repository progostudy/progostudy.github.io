The first step in dealing with the fallout from your actions is accepting responsibility, which is admitting that you were mistaken and in the wrong. Use when you intend to summarize paraphrase or quote from another source. Source material cannot make your points for you. Source material can back up your points or provide material for you to argue against; therefore, you will typically have to introduce source material and comment on how it helps prove your point.
