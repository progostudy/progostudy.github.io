tina = X

grace = Y

equation 1

X + Y = 27

equation 2

X = Y + 2

substitute eq2 to eq1

Y + 2 + Y = 27

2Y = 27 - 2

2Y = 25

In Bierce's view, life is a meaningless struggle against incomprehensible forces. Because it is futile to understand such imponderables, we should not bother trying.
