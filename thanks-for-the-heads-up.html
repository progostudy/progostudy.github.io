2. A motor going south speeds up from 80 m/s to 140m/s in order to overtake a truck headed in the same direction. The time it has taken for the motor to do this is 30s. What is the acceleration of the car?(short answer lng po ill rate the best answer 5stars thank you po)​


Answer: the acceleration of the motor is 2 m/s^2.

Step-by-step explanation:

To calculate the acceleration of the motor, we can use the formula:

acceleration = (final velocity - initial velocity) / time

Where:

final velocity = 140 m/s

initial velocity = 80 m/s

time = 30 s

Substituting the values into the formula, we get:

acceleration = (140 m/s - 80 m/s) / 30 s

acceleration = 60 m/s / 30 s

acceleration = 2 m/s^2

Therefore, the acceleration of the motor is 2 m/s^2.
