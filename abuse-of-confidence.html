14. What is child abuse?What is the solution for child abuse? ​


Answer:

What is Child Abuse?

Physical assault against a child is not the only type of abuse. Any sort of adult maltreatment that is violent or threatening to the child is considered child maltreatment. Neglect is part of this. Domestic violence is when a child is abused in the house by someone who is not the child's parent or caretaker.

What is the solution for child abuse?

Simple parental and child support may be the most effective strategy to prevent child abuse.
