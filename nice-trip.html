A Trip for Mike and Spike

By Robert Charles

1. The bright mice in the short story are named Mike and Spike. They are described as being always side by side which means they are always keeping each other’s company.

2. Mike and Spike are mice (or mouse in singular). Mouses are small animals that we can commonly find in our house, they are often chased by cats.

3. The mice in the short story is being described as bright and hardworking. Their brightness is hinted at in the very first sentence of the short story. Meanwhile, their hard work is shown through the rest of the story where they started their business and managed it diligently.

4. Before going on a trip, the mice Mike and Spike decided they should have money for their trip. In order to get their money, they decided to get a job. Their job involved ripe limes which they turned into a drink. After earning money, they bought a bike and went on their trip.
