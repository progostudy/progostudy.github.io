What is the author’s purpose in writing the paragraph?

A. To instruct

What text structure is used?

A. Sequence

This text describes a sequence of events or a list of steps

Signal words:

first, then, next,

last finally,

second Now,

before

Explanation:

Text structure refers to the organization of information. This helps students understand the text. There are five types of text structures. These are descriptive, sequence, compare and contrast, problem and solution, and cause and effect.

The author's purpose is his or her reason for writing a text. There are five main reasons why the author is writing a text. These are to entertain, to persuade, to describe, to explain and to instruct.
