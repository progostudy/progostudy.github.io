3. jona is going to visit her sister to new Zealand.he might leave the country at 9:00 p.m. monday and is scheduled to take 18 hours. What time and day should she tell her sister to pick her up at the airport assuming she need an extra half hour to deplane and pick up her bags?​


Answer:

If Jona leaves the country at 9:00 PM on Monday and the flight takes 18 hours, we can add 18 hours to 9:00 PM to find out what time she will arrive in New Zealand.

9:00 PM Monday + 18 hours = 3:00 PM Wednesday

However, Jona also needs an extra half hour to deplane and pick up her bags, so we need to add another half hour to the arrival time.

3:00 PM Wednesday + 0.5 hours = 3:30 PM Wednesday

Therefore, Jona should tell her sister to pick her up at the airport at 3:30 PM on Wednesday.

18. MY AUNT CALLED US UP TO MEET TO HER AT THE AIRPORT. SHE ______ARRVIVE THIS AFTERNOON ​


ANSWER:WILL ARRIVE

My aunt called us up to meet her at the airport. She will arrive this afternoon.

══════════════════════════

This is called Future Tense. We use this if we are talking about an event (in this case your aunt arriving at the airport) that is future activity.

══════════════════════════

There are 4 types of Future Tense. In this answer, it's Simple Future Tense, but we also have: Future Progressive Tense, Future Perfect Tense, and Future Perfect Progressive.

══════════════════════════

➼ What is Simple Future Tense?

This tense is used for an action or event that will happen in the future. It often uses the word will before the action.

Example:

I will go to the market tomorrow. I will see the new Spider-Man movie in December with my close friends.

In the examples, go and see are the actions and will is representing that they will go to the market, and they will see the new Spider-Man movie in the future.
