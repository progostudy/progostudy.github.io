19. Romans 1:16 For I am not ashamed of the gospel, because it is the power of God that brings salvation to everyone who believes Message of the verse?


Great question! First we must take a look at what gospel Paul is talking about. There's the gospel of the kingdom of God, and then there's the gospel of salvation, which is the one Paul preached, and the one we should be living by today. The message of this gospel is that christ died on the cross for the penalty of your sins, was buried and rose again the third day to give you everlasting life. Paul taught that, if you just believe that, you shall be saved. Nothing else required! That's the gospel of today. Now, notice there's a difference here between this gospel and any other gospel in the bible. Look at Romans 16:25. He mentions a "mystery" there, one was "kept secret since the world began". This mystery reveals dispensationalism. Make sense so far?
