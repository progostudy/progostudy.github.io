3. in a car racing, Pat gives Ron a 54 meter head start. After t seconds, Ron is at a distance 6t + 54 and Pat is a distance of 7t from the starting line. How far ahead of Pat is Ron after t secods?​


Answer:

(-t + 54) meters or (54 - t) meters

Step-by-step explanation:

Just subtract their distances after t seconds.

(6t + 54) - 7t = (-t + 54) meters
