3. meaning of hanunuo mangyan​


Answer:

A certain group of Mangyans living in Southern Mindoro call themselves Hanunuo Mangyans, meaning "true", "pure" or "genuine", a term that they use to stress the fact that they are strict in the sense of ancestral preservation of tradition and practices.

14. need the answer immediately please!! THIS IS AN ASSIGNMENT19. What does the tribe name Mangyan generally means?​


Answer:

Mangyan is the generic name for the eight indigenous groups found on the island of Mindoro, southwest of the island of Luzon, the Philippines, each with its own tribal name, language, and customs. The total population may be around 280,000, but official statistics are difficult to determine under the conditions of remote areas, reclusive tribal groups and some having little if any outside world contact.

24. 15. A Mangyan design comes from the Spanish word Cruz which means cross.  Ambahan Manunggul Jar Pakudos Design Pottery ​


Answer:

A pakudos or Pakudos Design

EXPLANATION:

A pakudos is a visual motif used by the Hanunuo Mangyan people of Mindoro in the Philippines. ... The word pakudos was coined from cruz, the Spanish word for cross. The pakudos motif is a common element in Mangyan embroidery and crafts. It is also seen on the official seal of San Jose, Occidental Mindoro..
