2. The purok 6 basketball team scored two less than twice as many points as their opponent. Their total score at the end of the game was 104. Let b be the score of purok 6’s opponent team.


Answer:

53

Step-by-step explanation:

Letting variables

a = Purok 6

b = Purok 6 opponent team

The statement

The purok 6 basketball team scored two less than twice as many points as their opponent

Equating to mathematical equation

a = 2b - 2

Their total score at the end of the game was 104

Equating to mathematical equation

a = 104

Substitute the value of a = 104 to "a = 2b - 2"

Substituting

104 = 2b -2

Solving further

2b = 104+2

2b = 106

b = 53

Therefore the score of the opponents team is 53

3c - 4 = 95

apply the APE (Addition Property of Equality)

3c - 4 + 4 = 95 + 4

3c = 99

3c ÷ 3 = 99 ÷ 3
