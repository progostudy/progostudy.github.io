4. REVOLUTIONThe earth moves along a path around the ___ This path kmow as an ___ One complete trip on this path around the sun is know as a ___ it takes the earth ___ to complete its journey around the sun. Every ___ we take the extra 1/4 days from the previous years, combine them all and add an extra day to the calendar called___Pls take a good answer I don't want to waste my time thanks you:>​


Answer

1.SUN

2.ORBIT

3.REVOLUTION

4.365 1/4 days

5.4 YEARS

6.LEAP YEAR

EXPLANATION:

1.The Earth moves along a path around the Sun.

2.Orbit, in astronomy, path of a body revolving around an attracting centre of mass, as a planet around the Sun or a satellite around a planet.

3.The act of revolving, or turning round on an axis or a center; the motion of a body round a fixed point or line; rotation; as, the revolution of a wheel, of a top, of the earth on its axis, etc.

4.For Earth to make one complete revolution around the Sun takes 365.24 days. This amount of time is the definition of one year.

5-6.To make up for the missing partial day, we add one day to our calendar approximately every four years. That is a leap year.
