2. explain this quotes " i wouldn't change you for the world but i will change the world for you".​


Answer:

This quote expresses a deep sense of love and commitment towards someone, while also acknowledging the need to make the world a better place for them.

The first part of the quote, "I wouldn't change you for the world," means that the speaker loves the person exactly as they are and would never want to change anything about them. This could be interpreted as a statement of unconditional love and acceptance.

The second part of the quote, "but I will change the world for you," suggests that the speaker is willing to take action to improve the world in order to make it a better place for the person they love. This could mean working towards social justice, fighting for environmental protection, or any other kind of activism or positive change.

Overall, the quote conveys a sense of deep love and devotion, while also acknowledging the need to work towards a better future for everyone.
