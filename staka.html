height of 100m

Solution:

PE = (9kg)(100m)(9.8m/s^2)

PE = 8,820 kgm^2s^2

\large{\mathcal{QUESTION:}}QUESTION:

A 120 inches of clay is to be molded to make a rectangular block with dimensions 2× inches high by × + 4 inches long by × + 3 inches wide. What is the length of the block?

\begin{gathered} \\ \end{gathered}

\large{\mathcal{SOLUTION:}}SOLUTION:

Using the Volume formula of rectangular solid.

Volume = Length × Width × Height

120 = (x+4)(x+3)(2x)

120 = (x²+4x+3x+12)(2x)

120 = (x²+7x+12)(2x)

120 = 2x³+14x²+24x

0 = 2x³+14x²+24x-120

0/2 = (2x³+14x²+24x-120)/2

0 = x³+7x²+12x-60

0 = (x-2)(x²+9x+30)

Get the positive root:

x-2 = 0

x = 2

\begin{gathered} \\ \end{gathered}

Now substitute the value of x to the dimensions:

Length = x+4 = 2+4 = 6inches

Width = x+3 = 2+3 = 5inches

Height = 2x = 2(2) = 4inches

\begin{gathered} \\ \end{gathered}

Conclusion: The dimension measures 6inches long , 5 inches wide , and 4 inches high.

\begin{gathered} \\ \end{gathered}

\large{\mathcal{ANSWER:}}ANSWER:

The length measures 6 inches
