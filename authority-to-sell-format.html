2. differentiate the Date Heading Format, Author Heading Format and Idea Heading Format. ASAP​


Answer:

The Date Heading Format includes the date at the beginning of the heading, followed by the title or topic of the written work. This format is commonly used in news articles, academic papers, and other types of publications where the date of publication is important.

Example:

April 25, 2023 - The Effects of Climate Change on Biodiversity

The Author Heading Format includes the name of the author at the beginning of the heading, followed by the title or topic of the written work. This format is commonly used in academic papers and other types of publications where the author's name is important.

Example:

John Smith - The Impact of Social Media on Mental Health

The Idea Heading Format includes the main idea or topic of the written work at the beginning of the heading, followed by any additional information or details. This format is commonly used in creative writing, such as short stories or poetry, where the focus is on the content and not necessarily the author or date.

Example:

Overcoming Fear - A Personal Account of Conquering Anxiety

9. what is format if the author is only one explane​


Answer:

If you are citing a book or reference with a single author, the format may depend on what kind of citation you will make. In paraphrase in-text citation, the format would be: Author Surname, Year. In quotation citations, Author Surname, Year, Page Number would be the format. And, in references, the format would be: Author Surname, First Initial. Second Initial. (Year). Book title: Subtitle.
