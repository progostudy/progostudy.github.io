Answer:

A bolo (Tagalog: iták, Ilocano: bunéng, Cebuano: súndang, Hiligaynon: binangon) is a large cutting tool of Filipino origin similar to the machete. The primary use for the bolo is clearing vegetation, whether for agriculture or during trail blazing.
