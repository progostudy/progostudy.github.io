26. What are some similarities and differences between e-mail and online chat?


SIMILARITIES AND DIFFERENCES OF EMAIL

SIMILARITY

The similarity between online chat and email is that both email and online chat are both used for messaging. It has the same purpose, to send a message to someone.

DIFFERENCE

The difference now between email and online chat is that email is asynchronous, meaning if you send a message through email, you don't anticipate any immediate response, while online chat is synchronous because the exchange of messages happens in real-time. When you send messages in online chat you receive a response in real-time.
