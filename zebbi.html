Answer:

1. 0.4 meter

2. 1500 grams

3. Camille, 1 hour and 30 min.

4. 28°C

Step-by-step explanation:

1. 3÷1.2=0.4

2. (1kg=1000g) 6kg=6000g

6000÷4=1500

3. Zebby (6:15-7:30) = 1 hour and 15 min.

36°C-8°C=28°C

5. 250 ml×4=1000 ml

(1000ml=1L)

2L=2000ml

2000 ml+1000ml=3000ml

3000ml÷100ml=30

Php 5×30 pieces=150 pesos
