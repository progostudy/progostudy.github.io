Behave in such a way that your father and mother have no anxiety about you, except concerning your health.

It basically says that the best way to honor your parents is to not do anything to worry your parents.

We really can't control if we have diseases (except for those that are due to lifestyle), so parents will always worry about our health. We can't prevent this. For example, any auto-immune diseases or cancer, nobody can really prevent this fully.

But aside from health, we should behave honorably and live a life that they can be proud of, and comfortable with. For example, what if you were a drug pusher? Of course your parents would be worried about you, they'd think everyday that you would be caught, or turn up dead.
