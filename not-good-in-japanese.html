26. 5S of good housekeeping and its Japanese Equivalent​


Answer:

5S or good housekeeping involves the principle of waste elimination through workplace organization. 5S was derived from the Japanese words seiri, seiton, seiso, seiketsu, and shisuke. In English, they can be roughly translated as sort, set in order, clean, standardize, and sustain.
