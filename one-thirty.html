14. 2. How to read 0. 1234?1. One thousand, two hundred thirty-four tenthsB. One thousand, two hundred thirty-four hundredthsC. One thousand, two hundred thirty-four thousandths. One thousand, two hundred thirty-four ten thousandths.


Answer:

The correct answer is C: One thousand, two hundred thirty-four thousandths.

To read a decimal number correctly, it is important to understand the place value of each digit. In the decimal number 0.1234, the digit "4" is in the thousandths place, which means it represents 4 thousandths or 0.004. The digit "3" is in the hundredths place, which means it represents 3 hundredths or 0.03. The digit "2" is in the tenths place, which means it represents 2 tenths or 0.2. The digit "1" is in the ones place, which means it represents 1 whole unit or 1.0.

When you read the number 0.1234 out loud, you would say "zero point one two three four" or "zero point twelve thirty-four thousandths."
