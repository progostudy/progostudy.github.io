10. Nous common ng proper​


Answer:

Common nouns name any person, place, thing, or idea. They are not capitalized unless they come at the beginning of a sentence. Proper nouns are the names of specific people, places, things, or ideas. Proper nouns should always be capitalized.

19. identify the common nou and proper noun​


Answer:

The difference between a common noun and a proper noun is what type of thing they are referring to. Common nouns refer to generic things while proper nouns refer to specific things. For example, the noun country is a common noun because it refers to a general, non-specific place.

30. what does nos mean in spanish


ANSWER:

Nos is an object pronoun which means: us.

It can be either a reflexive pronoun: nos despertamos

It can be a direct object pronoun: El profesor nos ayuda.

It can also be an indirect object pronoun. Mi madre nos da regalos para la Navidad.
