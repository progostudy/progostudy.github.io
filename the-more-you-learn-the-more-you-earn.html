Step-by-step explanation:

Word Problem  (Asked)

The given problem above is asking for the total earning of Mr. Fernandez for 45 hours in one week. There are three steps to find the answer.

Find his total earning in 40 hours.

Find his total overtime earning in 5 hours.

Add the two amounts.

Given:

regular rate per hour - 28.50

overtime rate - 1.75 of his rate

regular work hour - 40 hours

overtime work hour - 5 hours

Solution;

Step 1: To find his total earning in 40 hours, multiply the number of hours by his regular rate per hour.

40 × 28.50 = 1,140

He earns 1,140 on his regular work hours.

Step 2:  To find his overtime earning, we have to know his overtime rate per hour first.

1.75 × 28.50 = 49.875

His overtime rate per hour is 49.88.

Now that we know his overtime rate, let us multiply it by his overtime hours.

49.88 × 5 = 249.40

His overtime earning is 249.40.

Step 3: Add

1,140 + 249.40 = 1,389.40

Final Answer:

1,389.40

2.

Lucy saves ₱11,895.00 in a year.

Step-by-step explanation:

Money Word Problem

The problem above is asking for the total amount that Lucy earns in a year. There are two steps to find the answer.

Find how much she saves in a week by getting 15% of her weekly earnings.

Multiply the amount that she saves in a week by 52, since there are 52 weeks in a year.

Given:

Weekly earning - ₱1,525.00

Savings - 15% of her weekly salary

Number of weeks in a year - 52

Solution:

Step 1: To find the 15% of her weekly earnings, multiply her weekly earning by 0.15.

₱1,525.00 × 0.15 = ₱228.75

She saves ₱228.75 weekly.

Step 2: Multiply the amount that she saves in a week by 52.

₱228.75 × 52 = ₱11,895

Final Answer:

₱11,895
