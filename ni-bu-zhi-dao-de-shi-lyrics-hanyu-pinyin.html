When a syllable contains a single vowel only, the tone mark is placed above the vowel sound. ...

29. The Analects and Dao De Jing are sacred texts in Chinese religions. Is it true or false?​


Answer:false

Explanation:

The Dao De Jing (also known as Tao Te Ching) is a religious text of Daoism (Taoism, pronounced DOW-ism). The text is short, made up of 81 brief chapters. Its shortness, however, does not reflect its importance in the history of Chinese philosophy.
