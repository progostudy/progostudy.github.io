Life of Rizal

1. Which was NOT a reason why Rizal went to Berlin, Germany?

Answer: To publish El Filibusterismo

2. Which was NOT TRUE about Noli Me Tangere?

Answer: A. It was originally a project that would be made with other propagandas.

3. How do we translate the title Noli ME Tangere in English?

Answer: c. Touch Me Not

4. Which was NOT TRUE about the title of Noli Me Tangere?

Answer: a. The title served to confuse the readers.

5. Which was TRUE about the social cancer Rizal talks about?

Answer: d. It referred to nothingness.

6. What was the solution proposed by Jose Rizal in Noli ME Tangere to cure this social  cancer?

Answer: a. Education

7. Who was Crisostomo Ibarra?

Answer: b. A young man who studied in Europe and believed in a Catholicism to save his country.

8. Why was Crisostomo Ibarra considered an IDEALIST?

Answer: d. Because he believed in a certain utopia that must be achieved.

9. Who was Maria Clara?

Answer: a. A young woman who was narcissistic.

10. Which is NOT TRUE about Maria Clara?

Answer: b. She was the biological daughter of Padre Damaso

11. Who was Padre Damaso?

Answer: a. An arrogant and corrupt friar

12. Which was TRUE about Padre Damaso and the tinola?

Answer: c. He dislike the tinola because he received the neck and wing part.

13. How did Ma. Clara find out about her real relationship with Padre Damaso?

Answer: c. Ibarra investigated Damaso and found out about it.

14. Which was true about Elias?

Answer: c. He was considered as a lunatic.

15. How many times did Elias save Ibarra’s life?

Answer: c. three

16. Which among these statements could describe the dynamic between Crisostomo Ibarra and Elias?

Answer: a. They viewed life differently yet; they agree with one goal.
