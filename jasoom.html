28. Doctor Kaboom is skiing. He is not a good skier. If he has a mass of 103 kg, and an acceleration of 14m/s², what is the Force Doctor Kaboom feels when he skis into a tree?​


SOLUTION:

Step 1: List the given values.

[tex]\begin{aligned} & m = \text{103 kg} \\ & a = \text{14 m/s}^2 \end{aligned}[/tex]

Step 2: Calculate the force

[tex]\begin{aligned} F & = ma \\ & = (\text{103 kg})(\text{14 m/s}^2) \\ & = \boxed{\text{1,442 N}} \end{aligned}[/tex]

Hence, the force Doctor Kaboom feels is 1,442 N.
