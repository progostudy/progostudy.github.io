20. 14 Calum writes down his jog times for each day. Mon-15min Tue-10min Wed-12min Thu-5min Fri-No jog. He jogs constant speed of 9km/h. Work out the distance he jogs each day. On which day did he jog the furthest?​


Explanation:

distance = speed × time

where speed is 9 km/h and time is the time that Calum jogs each day in hours.

Monday: distance = 9 km/h × (15/60) h = 2.25 km

Tuesday: distance = 9 km/h × (10/60) h = 1.5 km

Wednesday: distance = 9 km/h × (12/60) h = 1.8 km

Thursday: distance = 9 km/h × (5/60) h = 0.75 km

Friday: no jog, distance = 0 km

Therefore, Calum jogged 2.25 km on Monday, 1.5 km on Tuesday, 1.8 km on Wednesday, and 0.75 km on Thursday. He did not jog on Friday.
