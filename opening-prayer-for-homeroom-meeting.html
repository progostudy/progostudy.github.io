27. what are the parts of homeroom meeting?


PARTS OF A HOMEROOM MEETING

• Opening Prayer

The meeting will be started with the opening prayer led by a teacher and parents.  

• Greetings

The preciding officer or a teacher will greet and welcome the students or parents in the meeting.

• Roll Call

The preciding officer will read the attendance and see if all are present or the body is in quorum. If it is already quorum the meeting is can be continue. If not the meeting will cannot be proceed.  

• Election ( if no officers yet)

If the body is in quorum, there will be voting of officers so that there will be a deciding body so that they are able to make their rules, policies and projects that will be implemented in the classroom.

• Reading of minutes

brainly.ph/question/280189

After the election of officers the secretary will read the minutes of the previous meeting to review what was done or talked about the previous meeting.

The content of the mintues of the meetinga re as follows:

• Date, time and location of the meeting.

• The purpose of the meeting.

• Names of attendees and those who were unable to attend.

• Agenda items.

• Decisions that were made.

• Actions that need to be done. This includes the deadline and who it was assigned to.

• Follow up meeting.

• Treasurers report

In this part the reporter will present the finances. The presentation of the Financial Statement preferably the Collection and expenses of the class for the period.

• Agenda

. This part of the homeroom meeting will determine the form and structure of the meeting so you can plan what will be accomplished.

key elements to a good meeting agenda:

Topic

Goal/s

Objectives

Non-objectives

Sharing essential information

• Releasing of grades

In this part the teacher will show the grades and release the card to the parents to show their progress, achievements and difficulties encountered.

• Feedbacking

The teacher will ask feedback from the parents regarding their children’s concern and the teacher will also give the feedback about the performance of the students.

• Other concerns

In this part the other concerns which is not included in the priority will be discussed.

• Adjournment

The closing of the meeting.
