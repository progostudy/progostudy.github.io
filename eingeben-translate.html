4. translate the quotient of h and three translate into their symbol​


Answer:

Word phrase:

The quotient of h and three

Algebraic expression:

h ÷ 3

Variables are symbols for numbers that we don not know the value yet. The 'letter' h above is an example of variable.

Examples:

When h is equal to 3...

h ÷ 3

= (3) ÷ 3 = 1

When h is equal to 36...

h ÷ 3

= (36) ÷ 3

= 12
