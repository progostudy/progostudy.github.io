1. what is the meaning of vain


Having or showing an excessively high opinion of one's appearance, abilities, or worth.Having no success or not producing a desired result

2. Ano tagalog ng vain at meaning ng vain?


adjective, vain·er, vain·est.
excessively proud of or concerned about one's own appearance, qualities, achievements, etc.; conceited:
a vain dandy.
proceeding from or showing pride in or concern about one's appearance, qualities, etc.; resulting from or displaying vanity:
He made some vain remarks about his accomplishments.
ineffectual or unsuccessful; futile:
vain hopes; a vain effort; a vain war.
without real significance, value, or importance; baseless or worthless:
vain pageantry; vain display.
Archaic. senseless or foolish.

5. what is vain world's uproar


Explanation:

O tell me no more of this world’s vain store,

The time for such trifles with me now is o’er;

A country I’ve found where true joys abound,

To dwell I’m determined on that happy ground.

The souls that believe in paradise live,

And me in that number will Jesus receive;

My soul, don’t delay; He calls thee away;

Rise, follow thy Savior, and bless the glad day.

No mortal doth know what He can bestow,

What light, strength, and comfort—go after Him, go;

Lo, onward I move to a city above,

None guesses how wondrous my journey will prove.

Great spoils I shall win from death, hell, and sin,

’Midst outward afflictions shall feel Christ within;

And when I’m to die, Receive me, I’ll cry,

For Jesus hath loved me, I cannot tell why:

But this I do find, we two are so joined,

He’ll not live in glory and leave me behind;

So this is the race I’m running through grace,

Henceforth, till admitted to see my Lord’s face.

And now I’m in care my neighbors may share

These blessings: to seek them will none of you dare?

In bondage, O why, and death will you lie,

When One here assures you free grace is so nigh?

23. what is the largest vain?


THE INFERIOR VENA CAVA

What is it?

- The Inferior vena cava is the vein responsible for carrying deoxygenated blood from the lower half of the body.

- The aforementioned vein carries deoxygenated blood, which is blood that has had its oxygen absorbed by the lungs, and directs it into the right atrium for circulation.

- It is considered the largest vein in the body.

- While the Inferior vena cava's size varies from person-to-person, with diameters that range from 1.2 cm to 3 cm.

- With the average adult size having the diameter of 2 cm.

- There is one particular case where a man who strained so hard while defecating, that he put massive pressure to his Inferior vena cava, and fainted.
