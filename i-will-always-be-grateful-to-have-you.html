14. 2. Why should we always be grateful to those who have helped us?​


Answer:

There are several reasons why it is important to be grateful to those who have helped us:

Gratitude helps to strengthen relationships: When we show gratitude towards others, it helps to foster positive feelings and can strengthen the bond between us.

Gratitude promotes positive mental health: Studies have shown that expressing gratitude can have a positive impact on our mental health, helping us to feel happier and more content.

Gratitude encourages others to help: When we show appreciation for the help that we receive, it can encourage others to be more willing to help in the future.

Gratitude is simply the right thing to do: It is important to recognize and be grateful for the assistance that we receive from others. It is a way of acknowledging the time and effort that others have put in to help us, and it is a basic principle of kindness and respect.

In short, being grateful to those who have helped us is important because it helps to build and strengthen relationships, promotes positive mental health, encourages others to help, and is simply the right thing to do
