17. an airplane, bound for city c, departed from city A with x number of passengers. When making a stopover in city B, 20% of these passengers disembarked and then 20% of the number of passengers that remained after disembarking boarded this plane. Assuming that 168 people disembarked in city c, the final destination of the trip, the number of passengers who took off on the plane from city A was equal to ?​


Answer:

Total passengers embarked in city "A" = 175 passengers.

Step-by-step explanation:

We should start by analyzing the statement.

The start of the trip is in city "A", there is a stopover in city "B" and the trip ends in city "C".

"A plane, bound for city C, departed from city A with x number of passengers".

Total passengers boarded in city "A" = x

"When making a stopover in city B, 20% of these passengers disembarked"

20% = 20/100 = 0.2

Therefore, 0.2x landed in city "B"

"20% of the number of passengers who remained after disembarking entered this plane"

After landing in city "B" stayed on the plane (x - 0.2x). And in the same city, 20% of this amount embarked:

0.2.(x-0.2x) = 0.2x - 0.04x = 0.16x

The statement also provides the total number of passengers disembarked in city "C": "Assuming that 168 people disembarked in city C, final destination of the trip"

"The number of passengers who took off on the plane from city A was equal to"

x - 0.2x + 0.16x = 168

0.8x + 0.16 x = 168

0.96x = 168

x = 168 ÷ 0.96

x = 175

We found the total number of passengers embarked in city "A" = 175 passengers.
