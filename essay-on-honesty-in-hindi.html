2. write an essay about honesty is the best policy​


Answer:

Honesty is the best policy, a phrase that has been used for centuries, holds true even today. Honesty is the foundation of any relationship, be it personal or professional. It is a quality that defines a person's character and integrity. Honesty is not just about telling the truth; it is also about being transparent, trustworthy, and reliable.

In today's world, where people are often tempted to take shortcuts and lie to get ahead, honesty has become a rare commodity. However, those who choose to be honest are respected and admired by all. They are seen as people of integrity who can be trusted with anything. Honesty is not just about doing the right thing; it is also about doing the right thing even when no one is watching.

Honesty is essential in personal relationships. It is the foundation of trust between two people. When you are honest with someone, you build a bond of trust that can withstand any challenge. On the other hand, if you lie or deceive someone, you break that trust and damage the relationship irreparably. Honesty is the key to maintaining healthy relationships with friends, family, and loved ones.

In the professional world, honesty is equally important. Employers value employees who are honest and trustworthy. An honest employee is someone who takes responsibility for their actions and is not afraid to admit their mistakes. Such employees are seen as valuable assets to the company and are often rewarded for their honesty and integrity.

Honesty is also important in society at large. A society built on honesty and integrity is a society that thrives. When people are honest with each other, they build a sense of community and trust that leads to cooperation and progress. On the other hand, a society built on lies and deception is one that is plagued by mistrust and suspicion.

In conclusion, honesty is indeed the best policy. It is a quality that defines a person's character and integrity. It is essential in personal relationships, professional life, and society at large. Honesty builds trust, fosters cooperation, and leads to progress. Therefore, it is important to cultivate honesty in ourselves and others. We should always strive to be honest, even when it is difficult, as it is the right thing to do.

17. Write a short essay about "Academic Honesty this New Normal Education". Use appropriate arguments and analogy in describing, explaining, and expounding ideas.


Academic integrity has been put into danger amidst the Covid 19 pandemic. It has become easier for students to undermine their academic honesty, such as copying others’ outputs, using the web during the tests, and asking surrogates to attend and produce their academic tasks such as assignments.

The transition to online evaluation of students’ outputs put immense pressure on teachers with insufficient time for curriculum adjustment. Many academic institutions had hope that everything would be the same as normal but things had gone the other way. The debate of intellectual honesty changed as face-to-face invigilated assessments were either online invigilated or converted to non-invigilated evaluations. Academic developers quickly prepared online academic integrity tools for teachers while finding ground in proven evidence-based concepts of academic integrity. However, despite the efforts of academic institutions, academic dishonesty had become rampant. Hence, it was a challenge for teachers how to appropriately give marks to the outputs of the students.
