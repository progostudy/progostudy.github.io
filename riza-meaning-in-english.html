21. Six years ago, Nilda was five times as old as Riza. In five years, Nilda will be three times as old as Riza. What is the present age of Riza?


Explanation:

6 years ago, Nilda was 5 times as old as Riza. in 5 years, Nilda will be 3 times as old as Riza. what is the present age of Riza?

Equations:

(n-6) = 5(r-6)

(n+5) = 3(r+5)

Rearrange:

n = 5r-30+6

n = 3r+15-5

Substitute for n and solve for "r":

5r-24 = 3r+10

2r = 34

r = 17 (Riza's age now)

solve for "n":

n = 5r-24

n = 5*17-24

n = 85-24

n = 61 (Nilda's age now)

5-6)ten less than thrice the age of Harry is 35.find harry age?

Let h = Harry's age

3h - 10 = 35

3h = 35 + 10

3h = 45

3h /3 = 45/ 3

h = 15, hence Harry is 15 yrs old

To check given x = 15

3h - 10 = 35

3(15) - 10 = 35

45 - 10 = 35

35 = 35

7-8)If the number is increased by 9 the result is 24.What is the number?

x + 9 = 24

x = 24 - 9

x = 15, hence the number is 15

To check given x = 15

x + 9 = 24

15 + 9 = 24

24 = 24

9-10)Riza is q centimeters tall.troy's height is 40 cm less than twice riza height.The sum of Troy and riza height is 290.how tall is riza?

Let 2q - 40 = Troy's height

Let q = Riza's height

q + (2q - 40) = 290

3q - 40 = 290

3q = 290 + 40

3q = 330

3q/3 = 330/3

q = 110 cm, therefore Riza is 110 cm tall

To check given q = 110

q + (2q - 40) = 290

110 + [ 2(110) - 40] = 290

110 + 180 = 290

290 = 290
