2. thesis statement about Lee Ji-eun "IU"​


IU

Name: IU

Native name: 아이유

Given name: Lee Ji Eun

Also Known as: 이지은

Nationality: South Korean

Gender: Female

Born: May 16, 1993

Age: 29

Lee Ji Eun, better known by her stage name IU, is a South Korean singer-songwriter and actress under EDAM Entertainment. Her stage name is derived from the phrase "I and You", symbolizing that people can become one through music. IU debuted on "Mnet M! Countdown" on September 18, 2008, with her debut single "Lost Child". However, she did not gain mainstream popularity until Dec 2010 in which she acquired immense fame from her hit song 'Good Day', earning herself the title of "Nation's Little Sister".

Throughout the years, she has built a formidable reputation as the "Music Queen/Goddess" with her consistent top-charting success, along with her heavy involvement in composing, lyric writing, and the production of her albums. To this day she remains the best-selling female solo on music streaming services and is one of the most influential celebrities in the entertainment circle.

Aside from her music career, IU has ventured into hosting radio and television shows, as well as acting. She debuted as an actress by playing a supporting role in the teen series “Dream High”.
