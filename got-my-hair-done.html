Explanation What is compare and contrast?

Compare is something that describes similarities between subjects. At the same time, contrast explains why the two are different. To compare, you use certain words to people, things, or ideas to show that they are similar. Also, it is the same as contrast that you can also use words to tell people, opinions, or things that they dissimilar. Â 

Examples of words that are comparing: also, as well as, both, in common, like, in comparison, like, too, same as, similar, likewise.

Examples of words that show contrast: but, as opposed to, differ, different from, contrary to, however, unlike, while and on the other hand.
