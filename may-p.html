19. Alvin saved P 1 600 in May. He saved P 2 000 in June. What is the percentage increase in Alvin's savings from May to June?


Answer:

It increased by 25%

Step-by-step explanation:

First you get the amount that increased

2 000 - 1 600 = 400

Then you divide it by the original price which is 1 600

400/1 600 = 0.25

Then you change it to percentage

0.25 = 25%
