9. What makes you doubtful to yourself? ​


What makes you doubtful to yourself?

There comes a time when I start to question myself as to whether I have done something well enough or whether I have succeeded in doing something I have never done before. Or it could be doubting a decision that I have made before and not feeling good enough to do it. This attitude is referred to as self-doubt.

Here are things that make me doubt myself are:

Listening to Negative Comments from People Around You

When I do something, people around me will criticize me. This will definitely make me doubt myself. What I have to do is to not listen to what they say. Because everyone has a different point of view.

Used to Needing Recognition

I am used to needing recognition from others, of course this will make me doubt myself. This is because I am used to making decisions relying on other people, because my goals also require recognition from them.

Past experiences or mistakes

For example the experience of heartbreak, being fired, broken relationships, and other bad experiences. Things like that make me doubt myself success in the future.

Childhood experience

Apparently how my development in childhood affects my habits and personality. If I am raised in a family that makes me feel inferior, then I also grow up like that.

Comparing myself to others

Self-doubt can arise because I am not satisfied with myself. Always look at the success of others, without intending to appreciate yourself.

Finding the right way to overcome self-doubt is not easy. However, if this one thing continues, it can cause anxiety or depression. In fact, according to the Huffington Post, over time self-doubt attacks not only our mental health, but our physical health as well. Some problems in physical health that can arise, including high blood pressure, weight gain, to heart disease. Self-doubt is indeed very destructive and can hold us back from achieving success.

Therefore, if you want to have a bright future, you must first get rid of feelings of inferiority within yourself.
