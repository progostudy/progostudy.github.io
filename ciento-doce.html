21. Guardia sereno-o-o a las doce han dado-o-o meaning


The line “Guardia sereno-o-o a las doce han dado-o-o” can be found in Nick Joaquin’s short story entitled “May Day Eve.” In the short story, this line was spoken by one of the watch men or night guards, who is probably the leader, and this line is a warning that 12:00 o’clock has come.
