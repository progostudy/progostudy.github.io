14. Whats the meaning of AGONSA?AGONSAFun Tuesday Math!​


Answer:

Asked, Given, Operation, Numbers, Solution, Answer.

Explanation

Agonsa is an acronym that provides steps for performing a mathematical operation.

Agonsa stands for:

A - Asked

G - Given

O - Operation

N - Numbers

S - Solution

A - Answer
