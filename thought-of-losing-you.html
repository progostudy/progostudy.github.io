Losing a loved one is indeed a very painful process to go through but we need to understand that every challenge that God puts us under to is for the betterment in our spiritual aspect. We may think that sometimes we are alone in the journey but little did we know that God is always in our side. Try to distract them with recreational activities that could make them less mournful. Be gentle always and think of all the things that you'll be saying. In short, show hospitality and care to your friend.
