19. 6 men hired to carry a big load with each one given 15kg. If each one is allowed to carry only 10kg,how many men should be hired to transport the load?​


Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

first, get the total load 6 men will carry.

6 men x 15 kg = 90 kg

then, divide 90 by 10.

90/10 = 9

since there are already 6 men hired, there is a need for 3 more men to transport the load.

27. How many sacks of rice weighing 25,000 grams can be loaded in a big truck that has a maximum capacity of 5,000 kg? 200 2,000 20 20,000


Answer:

200 sacks

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

Sack of rice = 25,000 grams

Truck capacity = 5,000 kg

Solution:

Conversion of grams to kg

[tex]25000 \: g \times \frac{1 \: kg}{1000 \: g} = \: 25 \: kg[/tex]

1 sack = 25 kg

5,000 kg ÷ 25 kg = 200 sacks of rice
