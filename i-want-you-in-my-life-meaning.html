Answer:

This quote by Peter De Vries tries to discuss the mystery of life by comparing it to a safe. As we all know, a vault or safe needs a combination to unlock it. The challenge here is that the correct answer exists, but it is trapped inside. There is no way to cheat by peeking inside because it is protected. By likening the secret of the universe to this puzzle, he is saying that it is hard to solve the mystery. However, it is not impossible. There is only one thing to do, which is to try different combinations and never give up. If you do this, it is only a matter of time before you crack the code. This is also true for life. As long as you keep trying no matter how hard it gets, you will get there eventually. When you finally get it right, you will understand and realize the way the universe works at last.
