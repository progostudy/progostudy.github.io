24. After charging your portable media player overnight, you turn it on only to find that it is reporting a low battery. Seconds later, it shuts off automatically. What might be wrong?​


Electricity is the source of the issue. Verify the wall outlet. Even though wall chargers don't usually malfunction, you should nonetheless examine them because sometimes the least likely thing to be the cause of an issue actually is. Try using a different wall charger. Wall chargers for Android also work with iPhones.

Look over the USB cable. Use several USB cables to test that yours is not broken as USB cables are typically the cause of charging issues. If your device recognizes the other USB cable, you've found the issue.

Look for lint in the wall charger port. Additionally, look for lint in the phone's ports. Lint can disrupt the electrical flow, making it impossible for your phone to charge. Stop by a service center for a checkup to see if your battery has already been damaged. In an Apple service center, checkups are free.

Change your battery if your gadget is still covered by warranty. It should be free. These are the typical causes of issues of a battery. The USB cord is the culprit. The USB cord breaks because we always pull on it.
