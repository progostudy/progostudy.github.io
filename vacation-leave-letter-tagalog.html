15. An american company is giving a 30 days vacation leave for contract worker of 2 years continuous service. John works in this company has extended his service to six years. How many days of vacation leave is he entitled to? in ratio


Answer:

90 days

Step - by - step explanation:

Since in every 2 years of continuous service grants a 30 days of vacation leave for a contract worker:

for 6 years = 3 (2 years)

Then, it will be 3 times the number of vacation leave in 2 years that would be the number of leaves for 6 years

30 (3) = 90 days

90 days of vacation.

6 / 2 = 3

30 * 3 = 90
