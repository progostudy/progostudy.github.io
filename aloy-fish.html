21. 1) what is percent by mass of sodium of bicarbonate in a solution containing 20g NaHCO³ dissolved in 100g of water? 2) What is the percent by mass if 8g copper is added to zinc to producr 100g of aloy? 3) What is the percent by mass concentration of a solution that contains 30g of salt dissolved in 300g of water? WITH SOLUTION ​


Answer: 1) Given 20g of NaHCO3/600g of water, so

Percentage of NaHCO3 in the solution = (100g/600g) x 20g = 3.3 g% by mass.

2) Note: An alloy is a solid solution. Brass is an alloy of copper and zinc. Copper is the solute and zinc is the solvent.

percent by mass = (mass solute / mass solution) × 100

percent by mass = (8.0 g / 100 g) × 100

percent by mass = 8.0%

3) Hence, mass-by-mass percentage of the given solution is 23.07 %.
