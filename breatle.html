29. Define breathing rate and breathing depth.


Respiratory rate(breathing rate) is the number of breaths taken per minute, which may change during certain diseases or conditions. Both respiratory rate and depth are controlled by the respiratory centers of the brain, which are stimulated by factors such as chemical and pH changes in the blood.
