30. Which lobe of the brain is located at the back, behind the parietal lobe?​


Answer:

The occipital lobe is behind and slightly underneath the parietal lobe. The parieto-occipital sulcus divides these two lobes. Like the brain itself, the parietal lobe is divided into two hemispheres by the central furrow, or medial longitudinal fissure.
