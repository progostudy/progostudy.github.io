2. The sum of ages of Mara, Hana and Ara is 60, and Mara is 60% younger than Ara. Eight years ago, the sum of the ages of Mara and Hana divided by the age of Ara gives a quotient of 1 and remainder of 2. How old is Mara, Hana and Ara?​


[tex]\mathbb{TABLE:}[/tex]

[tex]\begin{tabular}{|c|c|c|} \cline{1-3} & Present & Past \\ \cline{1-3}Mara & x & x - 8\\ \cline{1-3}Hana & y & y - 8 \\ \cline{1-3} Ara & z & z - 8 \\ \cline{1-3} \end{tabular}[/tex]

[tex]\mathbb{SOLUTION:}[/tex]

First condition: The sum of ages of Mara, Hana and Ara is 60

[tex]\mathsf{x+y+z=60} \longleftarrow \mathsf{equation \: 1}[/tex]

Second condition: Mara is 60% younger than Ara

x = 60%z

[tex]\mathsf{x=0.6z} \longleftarrow \mathsf{equation \: 2}[/tex]

Third condition: Eight years ago, the sum of the ages of Mara and Hana divided by the age of Ara give a quotient of 1 and remainder of 2

[tex]\mathsf{\dfrac{(x-8)+(y-8)}{(z-8)}=1+\dfrac{2}{(z-8)}} \longleftarrow \mathsf{simplify}[/tex]

[tex]\mathsf{\dfrac{x+y-16}{z-8}-\dfrac{2}{z-8}=1}[/tex]

[tex]\mathsf{\dfrac{x+y-18}{z-8}=1} \longleftarrow \mathsf{multiply \: both \: sides \: by \: z-8}[/tex]

[tex]\mathsf{x+y-18=z-8}[/tex]

[tex]\mathsf{x+y-z=10} \longleftarrow \mathsf{equation \: 3}[/tex]

Substitute equation 2 in equation 1

x + y + z = 60

0.6z + y + z = 60

y + 1.6z = 60

[tex]\mathsf{y=60-1.6z} \longleftarrow \mathsf{equation \: 4}[/tex]

Substitute equation 4 and equation 2 in equation 3

x + y - z = 10

0.6z + (60 - 1.6z) - z = 10

0.6z - 1.6z - z + 60 = 10

-2z = -50

[tex]\mathsf{z=\dfrac{-50}{-2}}[/tex]

z = 25

Substitute the value of z in equation 2 and equation 4

x = 0.60(25) = 15

y = 60 - 1.6(25) = 20

[tex]\mathbb{ANSWER:}[/tex]

Mara is: [tex]\boxed{\mathsf{15 \: years \: old}}[/tex]

Hana is: [tex]\boxed{\mathsf{20 \: years \: old}}[/tex]

Ara is: [tex]\boxed{\mathsf{25 \: years \: old}}[/tex]

7. Kahulugan ng mara clara​


Answer:

Mara Clara is a Philippine drama television series on ABS-CBN, which aired from August 17, 1992 to February 14, 1997. The series stars Judy Ann Santos and Gladys Reyes. It was the longest running ABS-CBN's weekday drama on Philippine television until 2020 when FPJ's Ang Probinsyano surpassed it with 1,217 episodes as of October 5, 2020. It was adapted into a feature film released by Star Cinema in 1996. Mara Clara was a first release from Dreamscape Entertainment. The title is derived from María Clara, a mestiza heroine in Noli Me Tángere.

28. mara mother bought a kilo of fish. she was asked to preserve it by smoking what are the steps Mara should do to preserve the fish?​


Answer:

Smoking Process

The process of smoking cooks dries and smokes the fish all in one.

Pre-smoking preparation

Before being placed into smoking trays, large fish may need to be reduced in size by cutting into smaller pieces so that there is more surface area for moisture to be removed during the smoking process. If moisture is allowed to remain it may cause the fish to deteriorate quicker and be susceptible to insect infestations.

Smoking process

The fish are then placed into the smoking trays with the freshest and most moisture-rich fish lowest down near the fire and the fish which are already partially dried, higher up the stack.

Storage

After the smoking process is complete, the fish will have a low moisture content and therefore can be kept or transported elsewhere for consumption. Fish can last from a week to a month, depending on the length of smoking.
