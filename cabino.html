6. carino vs people of the Philippines summary​


Answer:

Carino vs People of the Philippines, also known as the "non-delegation doctrine" case, was a landmark decision by the Supreme Court of the Philippines in 1922. The case involved the issue of whether the Philippine Legislature had the power to delegate its legislative authority to administrative bodies.

The Court ruled that the delegation of legislative power to administrative bodies was unconstitutional because it violated the principle of separation of powers enshrined in the Philippine Constitution. The Court stated that the power to make laws is a function of the legislature and cannot be delegated to other bodies.

The case established the non-delegation doctrine in Philippine jurisprudence, which remains a fundamental principle of constitutional law in the country. It serves as a safeguard against excessive delegation of legislative power and ensures that the separation of powers is maintained.

18. A mountain cabin on 1acre of land cost30,000 dollars. If the land cost 4 times as much as the cabin, what was the cost of each?


The land costs 4 times as much as the cabin. Total cost of both the acre of land and cabin is 30,000 dollars.

Let x represent the cabin.
Let 4x represent the acre of land.

4x + x = 30,000

5x = 30,000

5x/5 = 30,000

x = 6000

Evaluate x as 6000.

4x + x = 30,000

4(6000) + 6000 = 30,000

24,000 + 6000 = 30,000

30,000 = 30,000

The land costs 24,000 dollars while the cabin costs 6000 dollars.

19. 2. Twelve cabin train can transport 648 people. How many cabin train are needed totransport 1,944 people?​


Answer:

36 cabin trains

Step-by-step explanation:

From the problem, we can see that: [tex]12:648=x:1944[/tex].

Let [tex]x[/tex] be the number of cabin trains, which we need to solve.

[Sol]

[tex]12:648=x:1944\\\frac{12}{648}=\frac{x}{1944}[/tex]

Cross-multiply and solve.

[tex]\frac{12}{648}=\frac{x}{1944}\\12(1944)=x(648)\\23,328=648x\\\frac{23,328}{648}=x\\ \Large\boxed{x=36}[/tex]

From the solution, we can conclude that [tex]\bold{\purple{\tt{36\ cabin\ trains\ }}}[/tex] are needed to transport 1,944 people.
