15. A gold nugget has a volume of 20.0 cm3 and its density is 19.3 g/cm3. What is the mass of the gold nugget?


Answer:

Mass = 386g

Explanation:

Given:

[tex]V=20cm^{3} \\D=19.3g/cm^{2} \\M=?[/tex]

Formula:

[tex]M=DxV[/tex] where [tex]M=mass[/tex]; [tex]D=density[/tex]; [tex]V=volume[/tex]

Solution:

[tex]M=19.3g/cm^{3} x 20cm^{3} \\M=386g[/tex]
