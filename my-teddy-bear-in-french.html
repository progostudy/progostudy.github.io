10. what is the cost of manufacturing 50 units of teddy bears?


Answer:

Cost (A) of manufacturing:A=45y+90

Cost (B) of marketing:B= 3y² -- 5y + 25

Revenue (R) from sales:R=10y² + 20

answer

Step-by-step explanation:

1.The manufacturing cost is Php 2340

substitute 50 in value of y

A=45y+90

A=45(50)+90

A=2250+90

A=2340

2. The marketing cost is Php 7225

substitute 50 in value of y

B= 3y² -5y + 25

B=3(50)^2-5(50)+25

B=3(2500)-250+25

B=7500-275

B=7225

3.Total cost of manufacturing and marketing 50 units of teddy bears is Php 9565

Total cost for 50 units of teddy bear=manufacturing cost +marketing cost

Total cost for 50 units of teddy bears = 2340 +7225

Total cost for 50 units of teddy bears=9565

4.The revenue from selling 50 units of teddy bears is Php25020

substitute 50 in value of y

R=10y² + 20

R=10(50)^2 + 20

R=10(2500)+20

R=25000+20

R=25020

5. The profit from selling 50 units of teddy bears is Php15,455

Profit=Revenue-Total cost

Profit=25020-9565

Profit=15,455
