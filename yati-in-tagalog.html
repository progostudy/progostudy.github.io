6. 1. The temperature at Yati, Lilo-an, Cebu at 1:00 PM was 32°C. Between 1:00 PM and 5:00PM, the temperature dropped 3.2 °C. What is the temperature at 5:00PM? *5 points2. Jonathan’s body temperature is 38.5°C. After taking one tablet of his medicine, his fever subsided by 1.5°C. What is his body temperature now? *5 points


Answer:

1. 32°C = temperature at 1:00pm

3.2°C = temperature dropped / temperature at 5:00pm

32 - 3.2 = 28.8°C

So the temperature at 5:00 pm is 28.8°C.

2. 38.5°C = Jonathan's body temperature

1.5°C = temperature decreased after taking medicine.

38.5 - 1.5 = 37°C

So Jonathan's present temperature is 37°C.
