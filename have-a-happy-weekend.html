20. Do you agree on having school homework on the weekend why? or why not? ​


Answer:

It depends on the purpose and amount of homework assigned. If it is necessary for students to reinforce their learning and the workload is reasonable, then it may be acceptable to have homework on the weekend. However, if it is excessive and interferes with students' personal time and well-being, then it is not advisable.

30. 1.(Third Conditional) If the student_____( not be) late for the exam, they____ (pass)2.( Second Conditional) If she_____(have) her laptop with her, she_____(email) me3.(First Conditional) If the teacher_____(give) us lots of homework this weekend, I_____(not be) happy.4.(Second Conditional) If you____(not be) so stubborn, we_____(not have) so many arguments!5.(Third Conditional) If we_____( not have) an arguments, we_____(not be) late​


Answer:

1. If the student hadn't been late for the exam,they would have passed.

2. If she had her laptop with her, she would email me.

3. If the teacher give us lots of homework this weekend , I will not be happy.

4. If you weren't so stubborn, we would haven't so many arguments!

5. If we hadn't been an arguments , we would have not been late .

Explanation:

First Conditional- simple present + will + infinitive

Second Conditional - simple past + would + infinitive

Third Conditional - past perfect + would have + past participle
